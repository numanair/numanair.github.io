project: audiocontroller 5-channel mixer

3D printed audio mixer for controlling PC audio volume. Allows control of 5 or more programs simultaneously.

Designed to interface with deej, an open-source audio control program for Windows and Linux. For more info on the deej project, check out their work here: deej github.
